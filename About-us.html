HYS

Harrow Yoga Studio (HYS) is a not-for-profit organisation which runs Yoga and stress management courses. It was founded about 10 years ago by a group of Yoga teachers and others with an interest in Yoga, with the purpose of providing a dedicated centre for Yoga and related activities in Harrow and the surrounding areas
